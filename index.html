Onwatin Lake Stewardship Group

Onwatin Lake Stewardship Group - Protecting Our Lake!

Our Mission:

To protect and keep our lake healthy now and for future generations and to understand the issues affecting the water quality of Onwatin Lake and the Vermillion Watershed.

For in depth Information on Lake water quality and related programs feel free to vist the City of Sudbury website at:

http://www.greatersudbury.ca/cms/index.cfm?app=div_lakewaterquality&currID=660&lang=en

About Us

We are all in this together – and we are glad you are visiting our site. This is our online meeting place, where we organize our activities, share ideas, and publicize our mission. The Onwatin Lake Stewardship Group originated in 2007 with the first publication of our newsletter.

The Lake Stewardship Grant Assistance Program is funded by the City of Greater Sudbury through its Water Quality Program since 2005. Stewardship groups are directed to use funds on projects that protect and improve the water quality and natural environment of the lakes.

Contact us here.

Our Work

We hope to work together to keep our lake as healthy as possible. Whether you’re ready to lend a hand at one of our activities or have photos or articles to share about a recent event, here’s the place to do it.
